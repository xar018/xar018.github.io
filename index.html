ABOUT

I am Asmit Rajeet Datta, studying in class 12, founder of covrelief dwarka.

As the virus keeps all the people on their toes and keeps on destroying lives and families, there comes a need of vaccination, which is the only way of truly protecting yourself from the virus. Getting a slot booked for vaccination requires the usage of a portal provided by the government on the internet. In almost all localities, there are people who don't have access to such websites due to lack of amenities such as an internet connection or a device required to do so. These people are in threat of losing their jobs because many communities require you to be vaccinated to enter and work in the compound.

Covrelief Dwarka is an initiative to minimise the impact of the pandemic on our community by helping the under-privelaged population to book slots for getting vaccinated. Covrelief provides facilities for employers to book vaccines for their workers in a hassle free way, which only includes them to submit their aadhar cards.

Due to the rising deaths in India mostly during the second wave, many children have lost their parents, thus making it difficult for them to pay for their tuition fee and secure a future for themselves. This website also acts as a helpline for students who have lost their earning members to the pandemic, by raising donations for their tuition fee.
